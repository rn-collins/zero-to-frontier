About this project

Zero → Frontier is a free, independent educational reference — not a company, not a product, and not affiliated with or endorsed by any AI lab named on this site (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, DeepSeek, or any other company mentioned throughout).

The sourcing standard

Every claim is attributed. No claim is invented.

Every statistic, quote, and historical claim on this site links to a named, checkable source — a company's own announcement, a peer-reviewed paper, an established outlet's reporting, or an official product's own documentation. Where a claim comes from a fast-moving or secondary source rather than a primary one, that's said plainly rather than dressed up as more authoritative than it is.

Research method

Read in full, not skimmed

The underlying source material for this project's history and technique sections was read start to finish, not summarized from search snippets.

Verification

Links checked, not assumed

Resource links were checked against real, resolving URLs at the time of publication — see the disclaimer below on how quickly this space moves.

Neutrality

Debates presented, not settled

Where genuine disagreement exists among credentialed people — like the AGI debate — this site presents both sides rather than picking a winner.
