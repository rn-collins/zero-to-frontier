LEAD MAGNET PLANNER — RUN THIS PROMPT IN YOUR AI TOOL OF CHOICE
Zero → Frontier — free, ungated, no attribution required

HOW TO USE THIS: Describe your audience and expertise — the AI
proposes concrete lead magnet ideas and outlines the strongest one,
rather than you starting from a blank page.

---

I want to create a lead magnet (a free resource that attracts my
ideal audience in exchange for their attention/email). Here's my
context:

WHAT I DO: [your business/expertise]
MY AUDIENCE: [who you're trying to attract, be specific]
THEIR BIGGEST PAIN POINT RIGHT NOW: [what they struggle with]
FORMAT PREFERENCES: [e.g. "I can write, but can't design," "I could
record a short video," — be honest about what you can actually produce]

Give me:
1. FIVE lead magnet concepts specific to my audience's actual pain
   point — not generic ideas like "ultimate guide to X."
2. For each, one sentence on why it would genuinely appeal to my
   specific audience, not audiences in general.
3. Pick the single strongest concept given my format constraints, and
   outline it in full: a working title, 5-7 section headers, and
   what goes in each section.
4. One sentence describing the follow-up next step this lead magnet
   should point toward (not a hard sell — a natural next step).

Be honest if any of my five ideas is weak — don't pad the list with
filler options just to hit five.
