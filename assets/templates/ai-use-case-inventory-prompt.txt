HOW TO USE THIS: Copy everything below the line into ChatGPT, Claude,
Gemini, or any chat AI. Fill in the bracketed sections with your own
information first. The AI will generate a completed inventory table —
you don't fill this out by hand.

---

I want you to build me an AI Use Case Inventory for my work. Here is
my situation:

MY ROLE / BUSINESS: [describe what you do, e.g. "solo consultant,
marketing services for local businesses"]

MY RECURRING TASKS (list as many as you can, rough is fine):
[e.g. "weekly client status emails, monthly research on industry
trends, meeting notes 3x/week, drafting social posts, invoicing"]

For EACH task I listed, build a table with these columns:
1. Task name
2. How often it happens
3. What makes it slow (drafting bottleneck vs. research bottleneck
   vs. decision bottleneck — explain the difference if it's unclear
   which one applies)
4. Confidentiality level (Public / Internal / Confidential / Highly
   sensitive) — flag anything above "Internal" as needing a data-
   handling check before using AI on it
5. Reversibility (fully reversible draft vs. hard-to-reverse action
   like a sent email or filed document) — flag hard-to-reverse tasks
   as always needing human review before acting
6. Suggested AI approach (which kind of tool fits: general chat
   assistant, a research tool, a meeting-notes tool, a coding tool)
7. Worth trying now? (yes / no / not yet) with a one-line reason

After the table, give me:
- The single task I should try AI on FIRST, and why it's the best
  starting point (highest time savings + lowest risk)
- Any task where you'd recommend NOT using AI at all, and why

Be honest and specific — don't rate everything "yes, worth trying."
Some of my tasks may genuinely not be good AI candidates, and I want
you to tell me which ones and why.
