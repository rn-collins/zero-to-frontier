HOW TO USE THIS: Fill in your real details about the client/project.
The AI drafts either an onboarding sequence or a proposal outline —
pick which one you need, or ask for both.

---

I need help drafting client-facing material. Here's the context:

WHAT I DO: [your business/service]
CLIENT/PROJECT: [who this is for, and what they need]
KEY DETAILS: [scope, timeline, price if relevant, anything unusual
about this client]
TONE: [formal / warm-professional / casual — pick one]

I need ONE of the following (tell me which, or ask for both):

OPTION A — A 3-EMAIL ONBOARDING SEQUENCE:
1. Welcome email — sets expectations, confirms next steps
2. Information-request email — asks for what you need from them to
   start, as a clear checklist, not a vague ask
3. Kickoff-confirmation email — confirms start date and what happens
   next

OPTION B — A PROPOSAL OUTLINE:
1. Problem statement (in their words, based on what I told you)
2. What's included / scope
3. What's explicitly NOT included (to prevent scope creep later)
4. Timeline with milestones
5. Investment/pricing section (leave exact numbers as placeholders
   if I haven't given them)
6. Next steps / how to say yes

Constraints:
- Don't invent scope, pricing, or timeline details I haven't given
  you — use placeholders like [PRICE] if I left something out.
- Keep it specific to what I told you, not generic template language.
