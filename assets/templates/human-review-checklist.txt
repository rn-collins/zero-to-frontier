HUMAN REVIEW CHECKLIST FOR AI OUTPUT
Zero → Frontier — free, ungated, no attribution required

Use this before you send, publish, or act on anything AI helped produce.

1. FACTUAL CLAIMS
   [ ] Every number, date, name, and statistic has been checked against a source I trust — not assumed correct because it sounds confident.
   [ ] I have not asked the model for information it cannot reliably know (e.g., very recent events, private data about specific individuals).

2. ATTRIBUTION AND OWNERSHIP
   [ ] Any claim attributed to a person, company, or study is verified, not merely plausible.
   [ ] I have not presented AI-drafted analysis as if it came from a named expert or authority.

3. TONE AND VOICE
   [ ] The output sounds like me / my organization, not generic AI phrasing.
   [ ] I have removed filler, hedging, or repeated boilerplate the model tends to default to.

4. IRREVERSIBLE ACTIONS
   [ ] Nothing here triggers a send, payment, deletion, or public post without a separate, deliberate confirmation step from a human.
   [ ] If this touches money, legal terms, medical information, or someone's employment — a qualified professional has reviewed it, not just me.

5. PRIVACY AND CONFIDENTIALITY
   [ ] I have not pasted confidential, proprietary, or personally identifying information into a tool without checking that tool's data-handling terms first.
   [ ] If this was a client or employer's information, I have their consent to process it this way.

6. BIAS AND FAIRNESS
   [ ] I have reread this for any stereotype, assumption, or exclusionary language that a first draft might introduce without intent.

7. FINAL CHECK
   [ ] I would be comfortable putting my name on this exactly as written.
   [ ] If I skipped any box above, I know why, and I've flagged it to whoever reviews this next.
