Eighteen prompts. Paste them into your AI. It does the work.

These aren't blank forms to fill out by hand — each one is a ready-to-paste prompt for ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any LLM. You fill in a few bracketed details about your own situation, paste the whole thing in, and the AI generates the completed document for you.

How this actually works, if you're new to this: Copy the full text of any template below (use "Read on page" or download the .txt). Replace anything in [brackets] with your own real information. Paste the entire thing — instructions and all — into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or whichever AI tool you use (see the Tools pages if you haven't picked one yet). The AI reads your inputs and generates the finished output: a table, a policy draft, an email sequence, whatever the template produces. You are not filling out the document — the AI is, using your details as the raw material.
