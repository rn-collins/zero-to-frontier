Why trust it

The standard this site holds itself to

Anyone can claim to be "fully sourced." Here's what that actually means in practice, and where to check it for yourself.

🔗

Every claim links to a named source

A company's own announcement, a peer-reviewed paper, or an established outlet's reporting — not a vague "studies show." See the sourcing standard in full.

🚫

No account, no data collection, no ads

Nothing here is gated behind an email address, and nothing tracks you to sell you something later. That's not a marketing line — check the About page for the actual policy.

⚖️

Disagreements are shown, not hidden

Where credentialed experts genuinely disagree — like whether current AI is close to AGI — this site presents both sides on the Frontier & AGI page instead of picking a winner.
